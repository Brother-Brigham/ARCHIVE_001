# Brigham Powelson

Welcome! This site serves as my personal digital garden—a working space where I document my studies, cultivate ideas, and learn in public. 

## 🌱 The Digital Garden

If the portfolio above is the polished "front yard," the digital garden is the backyard. It's a web of interconnected notes, ranging from rough, half-baked "seedlings" to robust, "evergreen" summaries. Things here are always evolving.

Feel free to wander through the topics I am currently cultivating:

* **[[Theology & Scripture MOC]]**: Explorations of Latter-day Saint history, thought, tradition, and personal scripture study.
* **[[Academic Vault MOC]]**: Concept maps and synthesized notes from my university coursework (Political Science, Greek, etc.).
* **[[Creative Projects MOC]]**: Worldbuilding and project planning, including *Red Ivy Records*, D&D campaigns, and *WOTW* ROM hacking.

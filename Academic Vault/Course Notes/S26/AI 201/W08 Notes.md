## June 9 Class
**Roles and Tasks**
Elements of a TMF:
1. Role
	1. Title and Attributes
2. Task
	1. Describe task and subtask
3. Steps
	1. Describe all steps needed
4. Analysis
	1. Describe the output you want/need
5. Examples
	1. Provide one or more good examples
Role Prompting Recommendations:
- Use non-intimate roles
- Use gender-neutral roles

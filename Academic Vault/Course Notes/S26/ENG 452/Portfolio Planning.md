For the final assignment this semester, you are to create a portfolio based upon the work you have completed this semester and possibly previous semesters. As part of the assignment, you will need to tailor your resume toward two specific jobs you might apply for. The portfolio can be completed either as a website or as a zipped folder of your work. (Websites are way cooler.) Here are the basic requirements for this portfolio:

- Include two resumes, each tailored to a specific job that is currently available.
- Include at least four artifacts representing your own work. These might include
    - Projects from classes (may include group work)
    - Work created as part of an internship, job, or other professional experience (get permission before actually using an organization's intellectual property in your own portfolio)
    - A writing sample (yes, I only recommend one, and if you want to use fiction, make sure it is highly polished)
    - Amateur/hobbyist projects of professional quality (might include websites, book covers, D&D campaigns, or other writing or designs from personal interests)
- A short synopsis of your included artifacts. This may be done in a single summary paper or could serve as dividers that you might put in a physical binder to divide your artifacts.

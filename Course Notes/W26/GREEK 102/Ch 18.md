## 18α Story (last paragraph)
And on the next day, when it first became day, Philip, having gotten up and, behold, he was able to see; for he saw both the sky and the sun rising above the crests of the hills and the trees being moved by the wind; and he began to enjoy watching; for all of it appeared most beautiful to him. So, he began to hurry to seek the priest. And he, having seen him approaching, said, “Hello, boy; it is clear that the god kindly came to you. So, give thanks to the god—but go, to seek your father.”

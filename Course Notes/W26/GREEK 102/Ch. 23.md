## 23α Story
![[Pasted image 20260316121412.png]]
And the army of the Peloponnesians advancing arrived in Attica first at Oenoe, where they intended to invade. And when they encamped, they prepared to make attacks on the wall with both siege engines and otherwise; for Oenoe, being on the borders of Attica and Boeotia, had been fortified with a wall and the Athenians used it as a garrison whenever war occurred. And so they prepared attacks and they spent time around it in vain. And Archidamus received no small blame from this; for the Athenians brought in all in this time.
![[Pasted image 20260316121444.png]]
When they had attacked Oenoe and tried every way but were not able to take it, and the Athenians were not sending ambassadors to make peace proposals, then they set out from Oenoe and invaded Attica; and Archidamus, king of the Lacedaemonians, was lead.
![[Pasted image 20260316121512.png]]
And they, camping, they first ravaged Eleusis and the Thriasian plain. And after they advanced until they arrived at Acharnae, the biggest district in Attica of the so-called demes, and encamping themselves there they set camp and remaining there a long time were ravaging. And it is said that Archidamus was of such opinion that he stayed posting around Acharnae breparing for battle, and they would not have to go down the plain to battle them; for he was hoping the Athenians would march out against them and would see that their land was being ravaged.

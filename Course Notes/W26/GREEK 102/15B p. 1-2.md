### paragraph 1
Then all the night the barbarians were rowing this way and that, guarding both the straits and the escape routes, and the Greeks were keeping quiet, preparing themselves to fight. And when it first became day, the barbarians began to advance into the straits, believing that they are about to easily defeat the Greeks, and suddenly they heard the greatest shout--so that they were very afraid. For the Greeks, using order, were advancing into battle and sailing against the barbarians were shouting the battle song.
### paragraph 2

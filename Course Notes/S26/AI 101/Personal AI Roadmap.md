## Summary
I will use AI to better myself and be more efficient and skilled in all areas of my life, though I will not use AI to replace original work or sources.
## Plan for Use
I plan to use AI to enhance my personal productivity and to assist in filling discovered knowledge gaps. Whenever I use AI, I will attribute to AI whatever content from it that I have used. When I pull up an AI tool, the tool's output should never be the final product; rather, it should only be a step along the way to a final product. I will use it for images, text, code, commands, brainstorming, research, and scheduling. In my personal and academic life, AI has been and will continue to be helpful for decreasing the time I spend on task analysis and daily and weekly planning. By utilizing it to schedule my homework assignments and must-do tasks automatically over the course of a few minutes instead of doing such manually over the course of an hour or more, I can put that time toward personal development (practicing instruments, creative writing, etc.) or strengthening my relationship with my wife. I will learn how AI is used in my planned career field (US Intelligence Community, emphasis on cybersecurity) and begin to practice using AI in like manner. In classes where I am encouraged to use AI, I will use AI to help conduct research and organize ideas. In personal matters, I will use AI to research possible career opportunities and outcomes for my current education path, weigh pros and cons of different career choices, and to optimize and expand the ways I approach personal development.
## Limitations and Boundaries
I will not use AI to generate content that I then claim as wholly original and my own. I may AI to conduct research or outline ideas, but I will not use AI to create "final" content, content to be submitted, published, or otherwise shared as content created by me. I will not use AI output on questions of guidance or decision-making as the final word; rather, I will seek revelation on such matters while limiting AI use to the aspect of gathering quality information. I will not trust AI output implicitly--I will always attempt to verify information given by AI through the consultation of multiple models, multiple sources, and attempting to read through primary sources of information as often as possible.
## Growth Plan
I will spend time using different AI tools to become familiar with capabilities, to stretch myself, and to explore how AI tools can be helpful in personal productivity and areas of career and academic interest. Through the content of this course as well as greater exposure to AI tools, I will learn how to create more efficient prompts and will seek to develop better skills in verifying information. I will attempt to stay on the forefront of AI developments and news, so that I can adjust my approach to AI use as needed when advancements are made. In this way, I will try to always keep my AI skills relevant with current trends and career AI usage guidelines.
